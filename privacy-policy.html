Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 13, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Zipica. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our photo gallery app.

2. Data Collection

Zipica operates primarily locally on your device. We do not upload your photos to our servers without your explicit consent. Any data processed (such as photo analysis for duplication detection) happens locally on your device to ensure maximum privacy.

3. Usage Data

We may collect anonymized usage data and crash reports to improve the performance and stability of Zipica. This data does not contain personally identifiable information or any of your photos.

4. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@zipica.com.
