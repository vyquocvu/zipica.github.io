# Impeccable Design Context

## Design Context

### Users
Everyday phone users — not photography pros or power users. People cleaning up their photo library on the couch, at the bus stop, or during a lunch break. Both quick storage cleanup AND organized library management at home. The interface must serve two distinct contexts without feeling split.

### Brand Personality
**Light, clean, effortless.** Not aggressive, not technical, not flashy. The product feels like a gentle helper, not a powerful tool you have to learn. Premium but approachable. Think: a well-organized, sunlit room — everything has its place, nothing fights for attention.

### Aesthetic Direction
Refined minimal with warm undertones. Light mode is primary (people use the app in varied lighting — outdoors, commute, home). Dark mode available for longer sessions. Avoid AI defaults: no purple-to-blue gradients, no glassmorphism spam, no border-left accent stripes, no gradient text. The design should feel handcrafted for this product, not template-generated.

**Key visual principles:**
- Generous whitespace, unhurried layout
- Warm neutrals (not cold gray) — tint toward the brand's natural blue
- Restrained color use — accent is earned through hierarchy, not decoration
- Typography with personality but not performance — readable, warm, distinct
- Motion is subtle and purposeful — fast micro-interactions that feel snappy, not elaborate entrance animations
- Cards and containers used sparingly — not every piece of content needs a box

### Design Principles
1. **Effortless first** — every interaction should feel fast and natural, never like the app is showing off
2. **Hierarchy over decoration** — the most important thing should be immediately obvious without visual noise
3. **Light and warm by default** — dark mode is supported, not defaulted to, and should feel like the same product, not an inversion
4. **Whitespace is oxygen** — tight spacing creates clutter; generous spacing creates calm
5. **Motion serves clarity** — use animation to confirm state changes, not to add personality
